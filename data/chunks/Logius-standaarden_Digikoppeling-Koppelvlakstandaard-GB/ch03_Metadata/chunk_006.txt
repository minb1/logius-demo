- CHECKSUM_TYPE_NOT_SUPPORTED

- CHECKSUM_ERROR

- INCORRECT_FILE_SIZE

- COMPRESSION_NOT_SUPPORTED

- DECOMPRESSION_ERROR

- UNKNOWN_ERROR
